शार्ङ्गरवाद्यञो ङीन्।

शार्ङ्गरवादेरञो योऽकारस्तदन्ताच्च जातिवाचिनो ङीन्। शार्ङ्गरवी। बैदी। ब्राह्मणी।

No 1381. — Let ङीन्(ई-end) be the feminine affix AFTER the word शार्ङ्गरव “a Sarngarava,”etc., and after what ends with the letter 'a' of the affix अञ् (No. 1075) when the word speaks of a kind [not of a wife — No. 1356]. Thus शार्ङ्गरवी “a Sarngarava woman,” बैदी haidi [from bida, which, besides ending with the affix अञ्, as it is held to do when enumerated* in the list शार्ङ्गरव etc,” may end with the affix an] “a female descendant of Bida,” ब्राह्मणी। “a female of the brahmins.”
